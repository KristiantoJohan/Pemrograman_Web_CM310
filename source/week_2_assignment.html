My Experience As a Computer Engineering Student

As a 3rd year student of Computer Engineering, there are so many things i've ever done. A lot of projects and assignments have been finished really well and i also got a really nice score for about 4 semesters. But one thing that will always be remembered for me is when i won the national IoT Competition in Surabaya. It is the most wonderful thing i've ever had and that i will never forget.

I come to Surabaya with my partners, they are Bagas and Wahyu. We went to Surabaya by a train at 20.00 PM from Bandung and arrived at 07.00 AM. I think our adventure in Surabaya was quite interesting, even we had a little problem with the local security guard but i consider that as the part of our adventure along with our winning in the national IoT competition.
